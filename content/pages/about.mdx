# about this site

this is my corner of the internet. i've had some version of a personal website since 2004, and this is the current iteration. it'll probably change again. that's kind of the point.

## what you'll find here

- **[posts](/posts)** — longer-form writing about software engineering, things i'm learning, and occasionally things i'm opinionated about. i try to write the posts i wish i'd found when i was searching for answers.
- **[snippets](/snippets)** — shorter, more focused bits of code and knowledge. the kind of thing you'd bookmark or send to a coworker.
- **[projects](/projects)** — things i've built, am building, or abandoned with love.
- **[resources](/resources)** — links, tools, and references i find useful and think you might too.
- **[changelog](/#recent-logs)** — a running log of updates to the site itself. i like the transparency of it.

## what to expect

i write in lowercase. i keep things casual. i care deeply about the craft of software but i don't take myself too seriously. expect posts that are honest about what i don't know, code that i wrote at 2am and cleaned up later (or didn't), and a site that looks a little different every time you visit.

i write mostly about ruby and typescript because that's what i spend most of my time in, but i'm curious about everything and i'll wander into other topics when the mood strikes.

## why this exists

i believe in learning in public and sharing what you know. the web is better when people make their own things and put them out there. this site is my contribution to that.

if you want to know more about me as a person, check out [the meagan page](/meagan). if you're new here and not sure where to start, there's a [start here](/start-here) page for that.

# start here

hey, welcome! if you just landed here and you're not sure what this place is, let me give you the quick tour.

## who am i?

i'm meagan. i'm a software engineer and i've been building things on the web since i was a kid. i currently work as a senior dev consultant at [test double](https://testdouble.com). you can read more about me and my whole origin story on [my meagan page](/meagan).

## what is this site?

it's my personal website. i write about software, share code snippets, document projects, and collect resources i find useful. i also keep a [changelog](/#recent-logs) on the homepage because i think it's fun to track how a site evolves. more about the site and what to expect is on the [about page](/about).

## good places to start

here are a few entry points depending on what you're looking for:

- **want to read something?** check out [posts](/posts) — that's where the longer writing lives.
- **looking for quick code references?** [snippets](/snippets) are shorter and more focused.
- **curious what i've built?** browse [projects](/projects).
- **want to know how this site is made?** the [colophon](/colophon) has all the details.
- **just want to say hi?** i'm reachable at meagan [at] meaganwaller.com.

## stay in the loop

i'm working on a [newsletter](/newsletter) — it's not quite ready yet, but it will be a periodic roundup of what i've been writing, building, and thinking about.

thanks for stopping by. hope you find something useful or at least mildly entertaining.
